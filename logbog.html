Create your journal key.

Your journal is locked to an NFT on Cardano. Whoever holds the NFT holds the key — transfer it to hand over full access with perfect forward secrecy.

NFT details
This mints a one-shot NFT with your encrypted private key in its metadata. The NFT policy is locked after minting — only one token can ever exist.

⚠ This permanently transfers journal access to the recipient. Once sent, you will no longer be able to read or write entries for the transferred key.
